France
There was a major influx of Armenians to France as a result of the Armenian Genocide. By the late 1930's Armenian immigrants in Paris numbered 70,000. The growing instability of the Middle East in the 1970s brought new waves of immigrants to France, particularly from Iran and Lebanon. There are now some 250,000 Armenians in France, mostly in Paris, Marseilles, Lyon and Nice.
