This is your page. You can use this page to communicate with us or your fellow students. And you can come here to send us your ideas, and to see what others have come up with.

This page will always be open for new suggestions and creations. Click on the "Speak out" area to send messages or ideas you want to share with other students. In this page you will also read the announcements posted by other students and by the coordinators of the Narod Network Project. Click on the "See it all" area to access the entire list of messages.

By clicking on the "Send yours" area you can send us a drawing, story, discovery or anything else as an email message or an attachment. Here is your chance to express yourself on the World Wide Web!

The most current messages are listed below with the most recent message first. You can read the content of the message by clicking on its subject.

Be sure to type the title of your message in the subject line, and to include your name and age, the name of your school and your password in the first lines. Here is an example:
Subject: Translators on the Internet
Date: Mon, 2 Nov 1998
From: school@provider.com
To: student-bulletin@narod.org

Student - Vahakn Samuelian, 16
School - Armenian School
