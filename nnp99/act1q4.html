Visitors to the Festival will probably be in a festive mood, but they may still want to keep up with the latest news about Armenia and Armenains worldwide. Rather than provide newspapers, the Armenina News booth will have many online sources of information gathered from the Internet.

Task: Find the URLs of as many Armenian newspapers, magazines and newsletters as you can.

Hint: Try the following.
Go to the News and Media section of Yahoo and enter the search text: Armenia (but make sure to choose "just this category" to limit your search to News and Media.)
Go to Infoseek and enter the search text: Armenian news.
Go to the Armenians in Cyberspace Web site at http://www.flash.net/~hamoarb/armenweb.html and use the search engine provided there (typing "news" for example.)
Go to Yahoo and enter the search text: "International Newspapers Online by Country".
Note: If you are curious, you can check the "Links" in some of the newspaper sites you visit.
