Armenian Communities Worldwide
Armenians can be found in almost every country of the world. Throughout history, and for a variety of reasons, Armenians have emigrated from their homeland and established colonies in all parts of the globe. They have also migrated among these colonies, and continue to do so to this day. There are 7 million Armenians in the world. About half of them live in the Republic of Armenia.
