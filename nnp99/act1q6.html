Once out-of-town visitors leave the Mashtots Avenue Festival and Yerevan, and until they come back again, we would like them to keep in touch through the Internet. The Yerevan Web Page Contest booth calls for submissions of Web pages which have something to say about the capital of Armenian. We would like to show both visitors and possible contestants example of such Web pages that are already online.

Task: Find three or more Web sites about Yerevan, and tell us their URLs.
About the competition: In addition to helping us organize this or other booths for the Mashtots Avenue Festival, you are all invited to participate in the Yerevan Web Page Contest (as well as the Music Awards). Remember the Yerevan Web Page Contest submission requirements:

Submission One or more Web pages about Yerevan, created by you with the help of your instructor, including images, text, links and/or anything else that you would like to include. A simple example can be found at http://wotan.wiwi.hu-berlin.de/
