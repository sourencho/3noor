If people were to got to your city and close their eyes, they would hear many sounds. Some of them might be very typical for the city you live in. There might be a bell of a famous church, or the sound of boats or trains, or the sound of cars, crowds or a special location…

Try to listen very carefully to the sounds of your city, and record a typical sound in an audio file. It should be something special, not something you can find in just any city. But remember… no recognizable words, or obvious clues about the local language!
