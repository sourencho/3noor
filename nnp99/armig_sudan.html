Sudan
About 300 Armenians live in Khartoum, the capital of the Sudan. The Armenian community there was established in the beginning of the 19th century. The number of Armenians in the Sudan fluctuated towards the end of the 19th century, but then increased until the 1960s. The growing instability of the country in the 1980s resulted in considerable emigration, although a school, a church and cultural organizations are still maintained.
