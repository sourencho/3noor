Instructions for Activity I

It is snowing hard in Yerevan.

Seen from the window of the editorial offices of the Tsants daily newspaper, the city is all white. The streets are closed and the editorial staff has been unable to get to work. But the newspaper must be published on time.

The editor almost reluctantly turns to you. "We have seven unfinished articles that are missing crucial information. Here is a list of the missing data. We have very little time. Can you find what we need on the Internet?"

You say yes, smiling.

Here is the list of missing information. Try to answer the questions in one or more of these seven items. Click on each item to see its details, and jump into the Web to search for the data.
  1. Who is that Armenian scientist and inventor?
  2. What was that book... that field guide...?
  3. Komitas! What did he look like?
  4. Grandmaster? What is a Grandmaster?
  5. An Armenian tennis star not named Agassi?
  6. Can we buy earthquake information?
  7. Who photographed Irina?
