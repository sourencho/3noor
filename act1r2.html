1. We know the book is printed in 1997 and is available both in hardback and in paperback versions, but we need you to get us its title, author's name and publisher.

Martin S Adamian and Daniel Klem Jr's Field Guide to Birds of Armenia, published by the Armenian University of Armenia, can be found on the Internet at http://www.nhbs.co.uk.



2. We are told that two experts, Frank Gill and Keith Bildstein liked the book. Is that true? what did they say about it?

This is '...one of the world's newest and very best regional field guides' according to Frank Gill of the National Audubon Society. Keith Bildstein of the Kempton Hawk Mountain Sanctuary Association considers it '...an instant classic'.
