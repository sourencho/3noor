Welcome to the Three Pomegranate Network
The Three Pomegranate Network (3PN) links schools around the world in a collaborative network on the Internet. Through specially designed activities, students learn to use information and communications technologies to become acquainted with their global peers, to do research and to publish their work online.
In 1998, the first 3PN involved 10 schools in five countries. In 1999, we had more than 30 participating schools in eight countries. 3PN 2000 has now reached 15 countries and includes 70 schools, 35 of them in Armenia. Welcome to the Three Pomegranate Network.
