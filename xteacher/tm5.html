Narod Network Project email addresses
01.28.98

You have probably noticed that there is more than one email address that you can use to contact us.

Depending on where you are in the Narod Web site and what you want to send, the email message template that you open will automatically have the address where you should send your email message.

Let me give you some examples:

info@narod.org is the general public contact address.

nnp-linking@narod.org is the email address where students should send all their correspondence related to Activity I. Similarly, an email address will be posted for Activity II and then Activity III later on.

marielou@narod.org is the main coordinator's email address. Any email message that you want to send us and is not meant to be made public should use this address.

student-bulletin@narod.org and teacher-bulletin@narod.org are for the student/teacher bulletin boards. Any message you want to post on the bulletin board (student or teacher) should be sent to those addresses.

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact me (you know what address to use now!!)

Good luck.
Marie Lou
