Welcome to the Narod Network Teacher Page

Here teachers will share ideas and concerns, collaborate with each other on different aspects of the project and coordinate the work of their students with the work of students from other schools.

This page will always be open for new suggestions and ideas. Click on the "Post a message" area to send comments you wish to share here. In this page you will also read the announcements posted by other teachers and by the coordinators of the Narod Network Project. Click on the "See all messages" area to access the entire list of messages. To send a private message to the main coordinator, click on the "Contact us" area.
