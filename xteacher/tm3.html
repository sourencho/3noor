Suggestions on organization
01.16.98 We suggest that you divide your class into groups of students (i.e. four groups of five students each). Having a functional group structure will be convenient when Activity III - Presentation - begins. Groups can work in parallel on the same exercise, or can work on different complementary parts of a task.

Try to establish a routine for using email and accessing the Web. Perhaps a group of students can take responsibility for cheking the Student Page bulletin board and the Schedule for new announcements and events, as well as email received at the school address from the Narod Network participants or visitors. For cases where students have Internet access at home, they could access the site from home as part of the established routine.

Please make sure that you identify the email address exclusively dedicated to messages sent to and from the Project. This address will appear in the school contact information accessible from the World Map page.

Please send me your comments to all of the above as well as a description of how you intend to organize the class in a way which is appropriate to your particular needs and context.
