On user names and passwords
01.16.98 Once you have entered a restricted page (such as the Teacher Page) with your user name and password, you will not be asked for your user name/password again during the same session.

This is convenient, but it also means that you have to be careful to provide access to students through a separate browser session (by re-starting the browser) so that they cannot access the Teacher Page.
