This exercise said: "The Music Awards will consider all kinds of music produced by Armenian artists. For its opening presentation, however, we need one example of Classical music, and another of traditional Armenian songs. Can you help us find some samples from Aram Khachaturian and the contemporary artist "Anahit" (Anahit Goudsouzian) which can be listened to on the Internet? "

Task: Find the URLs of the Web sites where you can listen online to excerpts from the following pieces:

- Aram Khachaturian's famous "Sabre Dance"
- Anahit's "Kantche Kroonk" - Track 1 from her new CD of the same name*

Solution: The following search string, Khachaturian + "Sabre Dance", using Yahoo provided links to pages where "Sabre Dance" could be heard (click here to hear it now). One possible site is -

http://rcavictor.com/rca/hits/clips/clips.html

Using AltaVista with the following search string, Anahit +"Kantche Kroonk", you could have heard (click here to hear it now) Anahit's "Kantche Kroonk" on the following page -

http://www.total.net/~geghard/anahitcdinfo2b.html
