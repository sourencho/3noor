How do you get to school? What different means of transportantion do you use (walking, public bus, school bus, car… boat?) and what do you experience before, during and at the end of the trip?

Describe in no more then 200 words a student's daily trip from his or her house to your school. It should be one continuous sentence -- you can use commas but not full stops. You can refer to what you observe during this trip, like the kinds of trees or animals you see, the smells you smell, perhaps what you think about... But remember, no hints like street names or famous locations.

An example from our mystery city:

Like every morning, she is wondering about all those people busily moving back and forth along the street at that early hour, when the school bus arrives, with the name of her school written large in two languages on its side, picks her up and makes its way along the wide boulevard where policemen stand at intersections, telling cars to stop or go, ignoring the traffic lights, and then turns into a neighborhood of streets so narrow you would think it would get stuck there, passing noisy, crowded markets, chicken, rabbits and geese in cages, as she wonders about the smell of cooking already filling the streets, only to come out again into the wide and congested boulevard and finally enter the school yard where she finds herself in a different world, so quiet, calm and clean, compared to the city outside.
