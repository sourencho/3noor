"After inventing bedtime stories for Lori, I decided to start writing them down. 'Sail' is one such story. I tremendously enjoyed taking Lori to places that only existed in my imagination. She enjoyed the ride. I hope you do too. Most people enjoy kites as kids. I discovered kiting at the age of 37! Kiting can be a lot of fun. It can also be very relaxing. The kite used in this story is called a "Tri-D" and was invented in 1977 by Peter Lynn of Australia. Kites come in a variety of shapes, forms and sizes. Being an architect I find myself attracted more to three dimensional kites rather than flat ones. There are lots of nice books on kites. If you're interested in a list, just 'drop me a line' ( ha! ) " -Harout

Harout and his wife Catherine have illustrated "Darper" a picture book published in 1992. More recently he was the creative director for two art books published in the US. Harout is an architect and lives with his wife and two kids Lori and Armen in Northridge, CA. You can contact him at: Dedeyan@worldnet.att.net.
