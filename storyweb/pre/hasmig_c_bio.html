"I have a birthmark on my leg too! And when I was little, I used to tell stories about it... I chose the name Hratch because that's the name of one of my friends, and he also has a birthmark, except it's on his arm. I have always dreamt of going around the world in my small car, a Citröen 2 CV. I call it Doudoun. But Doudoun seems to want to stay where it is these days... I love all kinds of chocolate, except the ones with almonds. Ice creams too, of course. You can write to me at 6618@dm.net.lb and tell me what you think about my story." -Hasmig

Hasmig was born in 1970 in Beirut, where she attended the Nechan Palandjan Djemaran Armenian School. She then specialized in children's litterature at the Université Saint Joseph where she now teaches. She also teaches at the Khatcher Kalousdian center, and manages the children's section of a bookstore.
