Arutyun Khatchatryan, Mikael Dovlatyan, Suren Babayan, Atom Egoyan. These are just four of many Armenian film directors. The Film Ehibition booth will show a sample of Amenian cinema from around the world.

1- We need your help in researching the works of some Armenian directors.

Task: Visit the IMDb Internet Movie Database at http://us.imdb.com to find its list of 24 Armenian films and to collect the following information:

a) The title and year of the movie directed by Mikael Dovlatyan.
b) The genre, language and year of Suren Babayan's movie.
c) What movie did Atom Egoyan start shooting in September 1998?
d) The list of movies directed by Arutyun Khatchatryan

Hint: In the IMDb Web site, click on the link "more search options". This will bring up a page with different ways to search for a movie. Go down to the "Word Search" section at the bottom, type "Armenia" in the available space and select "Country" from the pull-down menu next to it.

2- Atom Egoyan will make a special guest appearance at the booth. Can you do some background research to introduce him?

Task: Find the URLs of two main Web sites that provide information on Egoyan. One of them contains the profiles of the people he works with. Who is Atom's vision of an "Armenian Princess" and his "sleeping muse"? Send us her photo and tell us more about her.

Hint: Go to Yahoo and enter the search text: "Atom Egoyan".
