This exercise said: "We hope that visitors to the Mashtots Avenue Festival always enjoy warm sunny weather. But in case rain is expected on their next visit, we would like them to come prepared with an umbrella. The Yerevan Weather booth will not sell umbrellas, but it will provides weather forcasts from different online sources."

Task: Find the URLs of up to four Web sites where we can get daily and weekly forecasts of the weather in Yerevan.

Solution: Weather forecasts for many cities around the world are available at a number of sites. Some of the possibilities for Yerevan are -

http://weather.yahoo.com/forecast/Yerevan_AY_c.html

http://www.rainorshine.com/weather/index/sites/almanac? site=&city=Yerevan+Armenia

http://search.excite.com/search.gw?
search=%2Bweather%2BYerevan+
