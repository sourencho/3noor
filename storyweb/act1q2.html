The Music Awards will consider all kinds of music produced by Armenian artists. For its opening presentation, however, we need one example of Classical music, and another of traditional Armenian songs. Can you help us find some samples from Aram Khachaturian and the contemporary artist "Anahit" (Anahit Goudsouzian) which can be listened to on the Internet?

Task: Find the URLs of the Web sites where you can listen online to excerpts from the following pieces:

- Aram Khachaturian's famous "Sabre Dance"
- Anahit's "Kantche Kroonk" - Track 1 from her new CD of the same name*

Hint:
- Go to Yahoo and enter the following search text: +Khachaturian +"Sabre Dance"
- Go to Altavista and enter: +Anahit +"Kantche Kroonk"
*Note: In order to listen to some of the audio files found on the Internet, you will need the "RealPlayer" plug-in for your browser. If you do not have it, you can freely download it from http://www.real.com/.
About the competition: In addition to helping us organize this or other booths for the Mashtots Avenue Festival, you are all invited to participate in the Music Awards (as well as the Yerevan Web Page Contest). Remember the Music Awards submission requirements:

Submission A short digital sound file of music, song or any other kind of audio composition created by you
Maximum length 1 minute
