Post your work for Activity I


If you are a participating student, click on the key button in order to submit your findings.

Be sure to include your name and age, the name of your school and your password in the first lines of your message. Here is an example:

Subject: Painting of Komitas
Date: Mon, 26 Jan 1998
From: school@provider.com
To: xyz@3noor.org

Student - Sanan Minassian, 14
School - Armenian School
Password - kaghdni

I found a painting of Komitas Vartabed which I am attaching to this email.

Sanan

For group submissions, the student name line can be omitted. If you have forgotten your username or password please contact us by clicking on the "contact" menu item on this page.
