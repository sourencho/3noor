Presidential Pictures

Say you need some professional photographs of the new president of Armenia. Can you find them on the Internet?

1. You should be able to find both black and white photos of the president speaking, and color photos of his inauguration.

Hint: Try looking for a good Armenian photo agency by typing "photo agency armenia" in HotBot.

2. How many other candidates were running for the presidency? Can you find their pictures too?

Hint: Try the official Electoral Commission site at http://cec.freenet.am
