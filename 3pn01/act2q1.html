If your group chooses this task, you will start looking for an item produced in your region that you think will be attractive to people around the world who shop at our site. You group will choose a product, document it and present it. You will need to come up with:

  1. The name & short description of the product
  2. A representative picture of it
  3. The estimated cost of producing it
  4. The name & short description of the region
  5. A map of the region

I am making a standard Collaboration Page that you will use to upload those five pieces of information.

Click here for a preview of the
Product Collaboration Page

As soon as you upload all the information for a product, it will be available for display in the e-commerce site. When all the products are uploaded and the marketing campaign is launched (see Task B) we will be ready to make our e-commerce site public.

First step: Begin looking for products and collectively choose one by April 21st.

Second step: Quickly put together a rough description and picture. Upload them by May 19th so I can give you my feedback through the Collaboration Page.

Third step: Make any needed adjustments based on my feedback. Produce the final version of the five pieces of information and upload them by June 9th.
