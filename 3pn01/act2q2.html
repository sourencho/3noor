The maketing side of our project is made up of two phases. If you choose this task, you will first explore the publications and other media outlets produced in your area, including newspapers, television programs, radio programs and Web publications. Then you will chose one of them and produce a real ad for our e-commerce site to appear in it. You will need to come up with:

  1. Your list of media outlets and your choice of one
  2. A representative picture, sound or video clip of it
  3. The kind of audience it reaches
  4. The cost of placing an ad in it
  5. An ad produced by your group for that outlet

I am making a standard Collaboration Page that you will use to upload those five pieces of information.

Click here for a preview of the
Marketing Collaboration Page

As you upload the information, it will be available for me and other students and teachers to see. When your ad is ready, and the e-commerce site is about to be launched (see Task A) we will try to actually place your ad in the media outlet you chose. Here are the three steps I want you to follow

First step: Start exploring and documenting you local Armenian media outlets and choose one of them. by April 21st, upload the list of media outlets, as well as the name, audience and advertising cost of the one chosen by your group. Also try to include a picture, sound or video clip of it.

Second step: Quickly put together a rough draft of your ad as a formatted text document, sound, video clip or Web page. Upload it by May 19th.

Third step: Make any needed adjustments based on my feedback. Produce the final version of the five pieces of information and upload them by June 9th.
