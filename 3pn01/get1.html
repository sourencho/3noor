Instructions for Activity 1

Hi. I'm Aren (that's me in the picture below). Before we start, let me give you my address so we can communicate. Here it is:

aren@3noor.org

I have noticed that the Web is, among other things, a large marketplace where people buy and sell all kinds of things in e-commerce stores. I think we can open our own online store to sell local products from different regions of our communities.


First we need to learn a thing or two about how e-commerce Web sites are designed. In this activity we will get a chance to take a close look at seven different e-commerce stores. I need you to compare them and tell me whether they are:

   A - Easy to use
   B - Attractive
   C - Sticky
   D - Quick
   E - Informative


Click here to find out what I mean by each of these five terms (specially "sticky"!)

OK, now you can click on each of the e-commerce categories below to go to a corresponding online store and do some pretend shopping. Your job is to give each of the seven sites that you visit a score for each of the five characteristics (A to E) mentioned above. Use a 1-5 scale to rank each site, where 1 means "not at all," and 5 means "extremely." Discuss your findings with your classmates and send me your ranking averages.
