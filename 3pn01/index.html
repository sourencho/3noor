Our theme this year is the borderless marketplace on the Web. We will explore e-commerce sites and launch one of our own. In the process, we will learn all kinds of things, from the art of online collaboration to the craft of creating a Web site. We will learn how to find local products, package them and market them to the world.
 


Exploring
  
In Activity 1 we explore e-commerce sites to see how some of the best ones are designed. We rank sites according to criteria such as "easy to use" or "sticky".
 

_______
Building
In Activity 2 we actually build a site and launch it on the Web! We find and document local products in our communities, and we market the site worldwide.
