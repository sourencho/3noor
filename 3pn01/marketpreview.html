A Preview of the
Three Pomegranate Market

Here is a sneek preview of our e-commerce site. It will be available both in Armenian and in English.


     

One of the main features of the site is that you can explore our store either by browsing geographically, based on the regions where different products come from, or by categories such as "jewelry" or "books".

   When shoppers clicks on a geographic area, they get a list of regions within that location from where we have products. As soon as they choose one, they are given a list of product categories or products to choose from.
   Alternatively, they could start by clicking on a general category, like "Food" and then pick "Spices" from the list. They will either get a list of regions to choose from, or the list of spice products that we carry.

Here is an example of what a product list might looks like. Clicking on any of the products will bring up a page with more details, a larger image and any additional information.
