Welcome to the Resource Center

The resource center is a growing collection of information, tools and guidelines for educators, parents and researchers. It includes a comprehensive searchable Database of Armenian Schools worldwide and information about our experience with Internet Videoconferencing in the Three Pomegranates Network.

The e4d institute Database of Armenian Schools

The Education for Development Institute has compiled a dynamic and searchable database of Armenian schools around the world. You can search for schools using a number of criteria such as location, name, number of students, etc.
