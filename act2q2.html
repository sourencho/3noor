Holiday and Custom
Here is what I found in a Web page about Armenian Wedding Traditions at http://members.aol.com/wedding389/atraditn.htm
Apparently this is how the elderly and even the dead used to participate in the pre-wedding preparations on the groom's side:

At the groom's house, wine carafes were opened in the presence of elderly relatives, and the pitchers brimmed over. The groom's father and a priest made a trip to the cemetery and prayed over the tombs of their ancestors. Then, the two men made rounds of the homes where relatives had died during the past year.
