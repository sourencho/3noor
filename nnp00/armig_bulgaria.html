Bulgaria
Between the 11th and the 19th centuries, Armenians migrated to Bulgaria, forming a community of 10,000 people. But after the Balkan War and the Armenian Genocide at the beginning of the 20th century, this number tripled. Now a community of less than 15,000 Armenians live in Sofia, Plovdiv and Varna.
