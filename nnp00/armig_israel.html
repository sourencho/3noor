Israel
Armenian colonies in Jerusalem date back to the 4th century, when Armenian pilgrims began to settle in the Christian Holy Land in large numbers. At the start of the 20th century, the Armenian Genocide brought much greater numbers of Armenian refugees to Palestine. Today, less than 2,000 Armenians live in Jerusalem, mostly in the Armenian Quarter of the Old City.
