Do you live in Yerevan, the capital of Armenia? Have you been there or plan to visit it some day? Do you think we need a good online guide to the city?... I do.


In this activity we will collect material from the Web to put together a city guide for Yerevan. Below are seven subjects that we will be addressing. Each of them is like an exercise which can help you improve your skills for doing research on the Web. Once we have all the information and material, we will build the city guide as a set of Web pages.


Choose at least 3 subjects from the list below. Click on each subject to see its details, and start searching the Web. Good luck! (Email me if you need anything.)
