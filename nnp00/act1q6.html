Can you find photographs of Yerevan on the Web? We need to illustrate the city guide with interesting images of the main sites of Yerevan, as well as pictures of daily life in the city.


Task: Find photographs of Yerevan on the Web. Try to find at least one related to a monument or landmark, and another illustrating life in the city.

Hint: Try one or more of the following.
Go to http://www.corbis.com and search for: Yerevan.
Go to Altavista, click on the "Images" button and type in: Yerevan.
Go to Yahoo and type in: pictures Yerevan.

Optional Competition: For the front page of our city guide, we would like you to create an image made up of a collage of digital images and drawings. Perhaps you can scan a ticket from a show, a train ticket, a poster or some other document and combine it with photographs found on the Web or that you took yourself. Or if you don't live there, you can scan photographs that your parents took when they went on a trip to Yerevan, or something from a textbook or brochure. Use these digitized images and a paint program on the computer to create some original work of collage symbolizing Yerevan.

The deadline for this Image File Competition is January 24, 2000. Participation is optional, but if you come up with the most interesting image file, your class can win a great prize.
