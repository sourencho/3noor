Are there many hotels in Yerevan? Visitors will want to have a list of the different hotels in the city and some information about them. They might also be interested in restaurants... unless they are lucky enough to be invited to an Armenian home.


Task: Find information about Yerevan hotels on the Web. We need information such as addresses, telephone numbers, prices, pictures, etc. Can you also find the Web page of at least one of the hotels?

Hint:
Go to any search engine (such as hotbot) and enter the search words: Armenia hotels.

By the way: What about travel to and from Yerevan? Maybe you can find some of the Web sites of travel agents and airlines which cover Yerevan. How can we reserve a flight to Zvartnots airport using the Internet?
