IMPORTANT NOTE:
You will see many texts in the answers which are either in Eastern Armenian or Western Armenian. But you should not use this as a clue about the identity of the text's author, because ANSWERS HAVE BEEN RANDOMLY TRANSLATED FROM WESTERN TO EASTERN ARMENIAN AND VICE VERSA! (Besides, many Diaspora Armenians speak Eastern Armenian too.) If you see an answer in Eastern Armenian, don't think it's necessarily from Armenia, and if you see one in Western Armenian, that does not mean it's not from Armenia. Tricky, isn't it?
