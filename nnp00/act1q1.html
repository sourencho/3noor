Will it rain in Yerevan tomorrow? Unlike a printed guidebook, our online city guide should be able to make predictions about the weather.


Task: Find the URLs of up to four Web sites where we can get daily and weekly forecasts of the weather in Yerevan.

Hint: Try one or more of the following.
Go to the "Weather" category in Yahoo, and click your way to Yerevan .
Go to Altavista and enter the search text: +Yerevan +"weather forecast".
Go to Excite and enter: +weather +Yerevan .
Go to Ask Jeeves and type: Will it rain in Yerevan tomorrow?

By the way: Are these predictions accurate? Why don't you check the online forecasts for your city (whether Yerevan or another) and let us know if they got it right.
