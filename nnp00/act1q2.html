How do I go from Republic Square to the statue of Sasuntsi Davit (David of Sasoun) in Yerevan? We can't put together a good city guide without a good map.


Task: Find the URLs of maps of Yerevan on the Web. Which one is your favorite? Can you download it and draw the route from Republic Square to a landmark like Opera Square?

Hint: Try one or more of the following.
Go to http://209.236.29.213/pages/maps/ and click your way to a large map of Yerevan.
Go to Altavista and enter the search text: : +Yerevan +Map.
Go to the Web site of Hotel Armenia and find a map of Yerevan with the hotel's location.
