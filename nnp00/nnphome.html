Exploring  Yerevan
In Activity I we learn about using the Internet to connect to each other and to do research. We will collect material from the Web and put together a city guide for Yerevan.
Identity  Game
In Activity II we use email and Web pages to play the "Identity Game" designed to acquaint us with one another and with each other's daily lives, values and expectations.
Family  Tracks
In Activity III we explore population movements using examples of migration in our families and neighborhoods. We collaborate to document both ends of migration routes we found.
