Welcome to the Storyweb

This is a site full of illustrated stories in Armenian for different age groups. New stories will be added to the storyweb all the time, after being announced several weeks earlier - so come back often. All the stories appear in both Eastern and Western Armenian.

The stories are divided into three series by age category. The "Hoond" series is meant for children up to 6 years old. The "Dzil" series is for 6- to 9-year-olds. And the "Hasg" series is for those above 9.

Please come in and take a look. But first, make sure you have the Arasan font. If you can read the white-on-red sentence below in Armenian, then click on one of the three age category icons above. Otherwise, quickly download the Arasan font and then come back. Enjoy!
