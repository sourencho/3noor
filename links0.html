On global online education
Globalearn mounts live interactive expeditions designed to engage children in learning and to build their awareness of the world community.
I*EARN is an organization dedicated to youth making a difference in the world through telecommunications.
ThinkQuest is an annual competition that challenges students to use the Internet as a collaborative and interactive teaching and learning tool.

On Armenia and Armenians
The Armenian Embassy in the US provides general information on Armenia's geography, history, culture, economy and political structure.
The Armenian National Institute is dedicated to the study, research and affirmation of the Armenian Genocide.
The UNDP Armenian Freenet provides low-cost and free Internet access and training services for non-profit, governmental and educational organizations of Armenia, as well as to Individuals.
World Vision Armenia World Vision Armenia is a Christian Humanitarian Organization that provides educational opportunities for disabled and orphaned children, creates employment opportunities for disadvantaged families, cares for children and empower communities through its relief and development projects implemented in Armenia since 1988.
